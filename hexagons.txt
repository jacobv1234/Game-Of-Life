known hex patterns (Bays rules)

2 adjacent - hex-blinker (period 2)

6 in a circle - hex-pulsar (period 3)

start with 6 in a circle, remove 2 adjacent - worm (period 4)

start with 6 in a circle, remove alternating - spinner (period 2)

hexagon with side length 4 - large hex oscillator (period 15)

Bays hexship (period 5, moves 1 cell orthogonally) - see paper
